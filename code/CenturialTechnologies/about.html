About Us
Centurial Technologies (Cambodia) Co., Ltd. is an enterprise focusing on the research and development, design and construction of intelligent building system engineering. It is authorized by Nanjing Yibainian Electronic Technology Co., Ltd., which has the first-level qualification for professional contracting of electronic and intelligent engineering in China. Established in 2019 Centurial Technologies (Cambodia) Co.,Ltd.

present, the company can undertake integrated wiring and computer network system engineering, monitoring system engineering, security protection system engineering, communication system engineering, lighting and sound broadcasting conference system engineering, smart card system engineering, garage management system engineering, information display release system engineering, and intelligence The comprehensive service of consulting, design, construction and maintenance of intelligent building engineering, including system room engineering, completely solves the worries of customers. The company has formed a complete quality assurance system in various links such as product selection, supplier selection, product procurement, project implementation, after-sales service, and product development.

At the same time, the company undertook projects and worked hard to develop itself based on local channels in Cambodia. It has become the general agent of Kstar brand in Cambodia and the silver agent of Huawei (Cambodia).
