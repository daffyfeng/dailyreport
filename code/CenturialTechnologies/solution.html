Service description
The holographic projection technology (front-projected holographic display) is a type of 3D technology, which originally refers to the technology that uses the principle of interference to record and reproduce the true three-dimensional image of an object. Then, with the guidance of science fiction films and commercial publicity, the concept of holographic projection gradually extended to commercial activities such as stage performances and exhibitions.

However, the holography that we usually know is not a holographic projection in the strict sense, but a kind of holographic projection technology that uses Pepper's phantom, edge blanking and other methods to achieve 3D effects.
